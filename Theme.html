THEME 03 : Basic Content

In this module we produced 2 short videos. First a short story on mobile phones and second an information video for and external collaboration partner. This module included the introduction to concept development using video, dramaturgy ,film language and media elements and recording and editing techniques . In the first assignment the was on the image and composition . Our work were subjective to a formative evaluation . In the second assignment we were focused on the communication with the video and video in a specific context. They were subjected to a summative evaluation.

THEME 04 : Basic UX Purpose

This module was the fourth and the last module of the 1st semester of the MMD programme. In this module we were able to get the basic understanding of and experience with how interaction between users and user interfaces works, as well as which theories , tools and methods we can use for research , design , testing and production of digital solution for smartphones so that our work was based on the specific insights about the actual users instead of our won gut feeling and untenable assumption. The main point of this was to collaborate with a case company that want to improve their users’ needs and usage situations into the design in balance with the company’s strategy, needs and goals for the design solution.
